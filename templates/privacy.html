Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. We believe in providing a completely ephemeral chat experience, without the need for users to sign up or create an account.

Collection of Information

We do not collect any personal information or identifiable data from our users. Our application is completely anonymous, and we do not require users to provide any personal information.

Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information or identifiable data with any third-party services, companies, or individuals. It is recommended to not disclose your private information into chat as malicious actors could take advantage of it.

Security

We take the security of our users' information seriously and are committed to protecting their personal data. We use industry-standard security protocols and encryption.
